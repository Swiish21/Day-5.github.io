Our Journey

Karibu Kenya is a locally owned travel company.
It was started in 2008 when two friends with the passion for tours and travels came together and formed the company.
They quickly learned and adapted from other travel companies, travel agencies and touring companies and today,
the company is among the best in the industry. It has established great working relationships with other top companies in Africa
and the world at large so as to offer our clients nothing but the best Services

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission is to make travel and holiday experiences great, stress-free & memorable.
We aim to do this by offering that extra service and affordable travel solutions.
To business and leisure travellers worldwide. For us the client is King and your travel or vacation is a journey that we make with you from the preparation stage
to the end of the trip.

Our vision is to be an excellent internationally recognised Tours and Travel brand
that every traveller in the world loves and will be proud to be associated with.
